Mark Elliot Zuckerberg (born May 14, 1984)is an American media magnate, internet entrepreneur, and philanthropist. He is known for co-founding the social media website Facebook and its parent company Meta Platforms (formerly Facebook, Inc.),of which he is the chairman, chief executive officer, and controlling shareholder.

Zuckerberg and his wife, Priscilla Chan, are spending $3 billion in an attempt to end, cure or manage all disease by the year 2100.
